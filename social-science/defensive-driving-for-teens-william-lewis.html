Defensive Driving Guide for Teens is created with the young driver in mind, perhaps just acquiring their learners permit or first-time drivers license. It is meant to address issues that are not covered in the state-issued driver study guide, to instill defensive driving techniques that are ordinarily learned over time, with experience. It is meant to STOP wrecks, STOP personal injury and lifelong debilitation, STOP lawsuits in regard to liable death or injury, STOP insurance claims, loss of vehicle, payout of deductibles, STOP loss of time from work or school, STOP a blemished driving record with corresponding increases in premiums, and STOP the stress and hassle that go with a wreck.
