Journalist and former diplomat Ronan Farrow writes that the US foreign relations policy has shifted from diplomacy to "shoot first, ask questions later." This is compromising America’s future.

Farrow stresses we can still do something about it, but will it be too late?

War on Peace is written by Pulitzer Prize winner Farrow who exposed in The New Yorker movie mogul Harvey Weinsten’s sexual assault allegations.
