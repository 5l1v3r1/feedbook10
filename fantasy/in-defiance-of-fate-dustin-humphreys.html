The exciting conclusion to the Waymaker series! Three years have passed since Arkaid first met the free spirit named Ravie. Now they, along with Ravie's sister Alie, will finally uncover the secrets about the prophecy that ties them all together. Many familiar faces return, some friendly, others decidedly not, to take part in the climactic final battle with the future of the world on the line. Recommended reading order for the Waymaker series: Little Bird* -> Free -> Trials of Stormrock -> We Need a Bruiser -> Alimiyah** -> Invincible -> Ravinaera** -> In Defiance of Fate *"Little Bird" takes place several years before the rest of the series begins. It tells a story that is mostly unrelated to the main plot of the rest of the series. As such, it can be read at any time without interfering with the overarching plot. **"Alimiyah" and "Ravinaera" are not official entries in the Waymaker series, but are nonetheless deeply connected to it and highly recommended in order to fully enjoy the story.
