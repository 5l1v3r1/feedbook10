Out of the Solitary Mind is a collection of fifteen poems depicting mostly of what lies on the dark side of imagination; portraying sorrow, loss, death, mystery, magic, and occasionally, pleasant things, it shows of how it is like to live in a solitary world filled with sudden and rather unpleasant surprises.

It may start with the pleasant story of a worm and a mango, but toward the end, it turns a rather sharp turn into a world of stirred emotions filled with the sorrowful fate of a talking bag, the pleading ghost of a royal princess, a mysterious phantom, a witch and her cauldron that everybody hates, and more.

Out of the Solitary Mind is guaranteed to take the mind on a wild ride on a path twisted with emotions, fear, and the less-garish.
