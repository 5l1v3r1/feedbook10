Building a Bridge, One Prayer at a Time is an extension of a Bible studygroup that started on a bridge outside author Tini Siders office. She andher coworkers began bridging the gap for others through prayer, formingthe Bridge Prayer Group. This spiritual guide shares weekly devotions thatwill enhance your relationship with Christ as a result of spending timewith Him each week, helping you to become a bridge builder, as well.

Because these devotions are the result of the authors stream of consciousnessas she ponders what God has shown her during the week, they mayappear to be somewhat random in nature. Presented in a casual, relaxedformat, they can be read as if you are having a conversation with a friend;make yourself comfortable in sharing your thoughts with God throughprayer. You can also write your thoughts and prayers as you meditate onHis Word, and, most importantly, try to put into action the things theHoly Spirit brings to your mind as you study.

Build bridges and allow God to change your lifeyou will never regret it.
