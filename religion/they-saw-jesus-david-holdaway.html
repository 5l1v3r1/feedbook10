They Saw Jesus is a fascinating book detailing modern day accounts of face to face encounters with Jesus Christ.

It includes accounts about Fred Lemon, Doreen Irving, Smith Wigglesworth, Gulshan Esther, Betty Baxter, David Yonggi Cho, Sundar Singh, Richard Wurmbrandt, Samuel Doctorian and many others. 

These are not just spiritual experiences of Jesus’ presence but actual physical encounters where dramatic and dynamic conversions, healings and deliverances took place. 

There are both individual encounters and group encounters when Jesus was seen by many people at the same time such as at a school in Arabia, a church in California and an open air meeting in China.

This book is a faith building inspiration that is s breath of fresh air. It shows that Jesus Christ is as real today as He was 2000 years ago and is still in the business of changing lives.

Also included in the book are appendices which discuss issues related to visions such as: Discernment, Visions and Dreams, Visions and Encounters in Church History, Explaining Visions and Encounters with Jesus and Interpretations of Visions and Encounters. This is a helpful discussion and insight into the whole area of visions and dream and how this can relate to the 

Christian faith.

This book will not only stimulate your faith and love for God but will cause you to worship and draw closer to Him.
