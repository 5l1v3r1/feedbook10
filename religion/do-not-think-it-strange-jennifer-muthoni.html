Christians are called to follow Christ in His suffering, sometimes by suffering persecution, and always by facing life’s many difficulties.

The Apostle Peter declares: in 1 Peter 4:12-19 (ASV), “Beloved, think it not strange concerning the fiery trial among you, which cometh upon you to prove you, as though a strange thing happened unto you: but insomuch as ye are partakers of Christ’s suerings, rejoice; that at the revelation of his glory also ye may rejoice with exceeding joy.”

In this book, Jennifer Muthoni takes the reader through God’s scriptural blueprints for the suffering Church, and reveals the victory which every believer can experience through the triumph of Christ.

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Jennifer Muthoni is a retired nurse and a mother blessed with four adult children. She is active in her local church, and passionate to see the body of Christ hunger for more of God.
