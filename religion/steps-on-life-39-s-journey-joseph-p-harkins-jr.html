Steps on Lifes Journey: A Beginners Guide is a how-to guide for the new believer in Jesus Christ. It teaches you about the important stepping stones to build your relationship with your Lord and Savior through the use of Scripture and the authors own personal testimony. It walks through beginning faith-building basics, such as dispelling common myths and misconceptions about your faith, preparing for spiritual battle with the armor of God, tithing, and serving and witnessing to others. Each chapter heading is a question, and the chapter answers that question in an easy-to-read fashion. Each chapter then ends with a summarized answer of the question.
