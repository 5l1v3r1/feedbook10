The Legacy of Adam Kingston is so much more than a novel of romance. It's a story of building lives, having to say goodbye, and facing futures that are uncertain, but carrying that love on. It's about finding that road that we may be walking down and share it with someone special that will take our hand and walk with us. Megan had dealt with death, Adam was running from it. Together they built a life that was filled with devotion, friendships, and a successful business. They were respected and admired by people everywhere, ashore as well as on the open seas. Adam held a secret so painful, and Megan knew he wasn't going to share that secret, until her very life was at stake. Her life, and the life of their unborn child. They both had to rely on Adam's insufferable step-son for the answers. Then after a tragic happening , Simon changed and began to become the man, the Kingston, Adam always hoped he would be, but it may be to late. Not the police or the security hired could stop Adam from confronting his past face to face. Everything Adam cherished was at risk, was in danger, he knew he had to fight with every breath to protect his life, his family his "Angel" that would be Adam's Legacy...
