(An Urban Monarch Novel book 1) Mature Theme 18+ Genevieve is done school for the summer. It is her first summer back since she left all those years ago. She arrives at her father's summer house and plans to do nothing but party all summer long. That is until she runs into a family friend who she's been in love with since she was ten. Callum is sent by Eve's father to watch over her for the summer. He hasn't seen her since she was thirteen and shipped off to boarding school in order to keep her away from her father's business and all the dangers that come with it. Once he lays eyes on her all grown up he can't seem to stay away from her even though he knows she's off limits. They have an unquenchable desire to be with one another but are being forced apart at every turn. Can their desire survive or will Callum's dangerous lifestyle prove to be too much?
