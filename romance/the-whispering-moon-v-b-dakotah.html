It's known as the Souls' Amulet, and it has the power to rip its bearers apart. When Raychel Vagan, a high school junior like any other, finds a boy bleeding and unconscious on the sidewalk, everything she knows changes. With no one else around to help the stranger, Raychel takes him in, but something clearly isn't normal about him, or the amulet hanging from his throat. After getting bandaged up, the boy vanishes, leaving the necklace, and Raychel, behind. When this mysterious boy shows up again in her class with his pack of bizarre friends, Raychel's life is thrown into a world of darkness, evil, monsters, and secrets...one of which could damn her soul to a fate worse than Hell itself. In a world full of twisted love, hate, and death, sometimes the only ones that can be trusted are the demons within.
