Everything you need to pass the Praxis Core Academic Skills for Educators series of exams! The Praxis Core Academic Skills for Educators tests, which assess your basic skills in Reading, Writing, and Mathematics, are designed to see whether you have the academic skills to be an effective teacher. Colleges and universities may use these tests to evaluate individuals for entry into teacher education programs, and many states also require Praxis Core scores as part of their teacher licensing process. Praxis Core Academic Skills for Educators, which provides access to 12 practice tests, is designed for test takers studying for the: • Core Academic Skills for Educators: Reading (5712) • Core Academic Skills for Educators: Writing (5722) • Core Academic Skills for Educators: Mathematics (5732) Inside you will find comprehensive preparation for all three tests, including: • Tips on how to tackle all reading comprehension questions, including finding the main idea, drawing conclusions, identifying key details, and understanding figurative language • A thorough review of all math you need to know for the exam: numbers and quantity, algebra and functions, geometry, statistics, and probability • Information on how to conquer writing skills, including grammatical relationships, research skills, and language mechanics • A step-by-step process on how to manage your time to write high-scoring essays • 3 full-length practice exams for EACH subject, and online access to three more • A FREE online practice exam for every Praxis Core test!
