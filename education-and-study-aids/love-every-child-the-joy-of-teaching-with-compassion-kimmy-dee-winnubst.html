Teaching isn’t an easy job. But it can be a great one.

It’s all a matter of attitude. And with Love Every Child, veteran teacher Kimmy Dee Winnubst is out to help you change yours, and become the best, most effective, and most-importantly the happiest teacher you can be.

Packed with fresh ideas, inspiration and real-life schoolroom survival tips gleaned over a 30-year teaching career, Love Every Child is every teacher’s guide to excelling in the classroom and loving their work by approaching every child with a kind heart and an open mind. Whether this is your first year as an educator or your 40th, you’ll learn:

  • New ideas for reaching even the most difficult students
  • How to use humor to keep control in the classroom
  • Secrets to keeping the job fresh
  • How to transform your attitude by transforming your environment
  • Essential self-care tips for teachers
  • Visualization techniques, helpful exercises, and much, much more
