How do you protect your family? At what level of importance are the members of your extended family? The intent is to use all of your resources available and a great way to re-energize your relationship with them other than the yearly family reunion. It is the strategy of the family to have contingency plans developed and maintained to address emergency/ disaster situations to ensure the safety of all and allow for the prompt restoration of personal operations at family owned/ leased facilities. With correctly implemented planning, emergency response, incident management, crisis management, disaster recovery, and personal recovery plans developed, maintained, and tested will keep your family safe. Could you survive and protect your family through a disaster or disruption? The intent of this book is to give you the life skills needed to evacuate, shelter in place, and lock down, navigate to a place of safety, recover from the incident, and get back to a normalize lifestyle.
