Seventh grade halfback, Scott Weston, lives, eats, and breathes football – and dreams to play big. He joins a hundred other boys for an intense, four-week summer training camp at Putnam Middle School.

It all begins as a normal camp, until one knockout punch on the field changes it all. Through ways, nothing short of extraordinary, Scott finds out that all of them have been primed – exposed to chemicals that boost their strength and speed… as well as enhance sexual experiences.

The eighth graders tip Scott and his friends off about something special that’s about to happen, but, even these older boys aren’t prepared for what awaits them – only their sultry school nurse holds the key to unlocking their true power.
