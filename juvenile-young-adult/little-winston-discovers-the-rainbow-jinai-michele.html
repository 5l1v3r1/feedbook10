You will fall in love with Little Winston Discovers the Rainbow, a beautifully written and illustrated children’s book that reminds readers of all ages that God’s love is ever present, even during life’s difficulties.  

After receiving disappointing news, Little Winston, a seven-year-old orphan, runs away from his foster home to the creek outside of town. At the creek, God reveals himself and uses the colors of the rainbow to fill Little Winston’s broken heart with love. “I AM wherever you are. Just look for the rainbow.”

Jinai Michele pays homage to her one surviving grandparent, her grandfather, by naming the main character in this fictional story after him. Her grandfather, Winston “Action” Jackson, Sr., was a member of the 2nd Ranger Infantry Company (Airborne), the only all-black Ranger unit in United States Army history. The unit was active during the Korean War.

The book is available in three formats—e-book, paperback, and hardcover—and can be purchased at www.jinaimichele.com and online bookstores.
