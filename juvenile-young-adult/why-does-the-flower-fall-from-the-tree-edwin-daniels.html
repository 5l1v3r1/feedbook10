Why Does the Flower Fall From the Tree? is a sweet story about an adventurous, little girl name Sara and her search to find the reason why a small flower has fallen from its place on a tree to the ground. She follows the winding trail of events that lead up to the flower's fall, and interacts with all of the creatures and elements that had a hand in it. Sara meets many beautiful and strange creatures along the way, as she develops a more holistic view of the world that she lives in. As she traces back the steps, she is unknowingly learning a valuable lesson about God's hand at work in the world, as well as His love for His creation. Your child will travel with Sara as she learns the importance of every member of God's creation, as well as the worth of all living things in the Creator's eyes.
