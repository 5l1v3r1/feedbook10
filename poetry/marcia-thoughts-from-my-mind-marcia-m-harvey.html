MooreLove Ministries presentsMarcia: Thought s From My Mind. This is the last book of the trilogy series. Thoughts From My Mind is a compilation of poems from Marcias womanhood. It is about learning lifes lessons and discovering Gods immeasurable and unconditional love, grace and mercy. MooreLove Ministries is a family organization, orchestrated by God, to reach people in thought, actions, and deeds. God has gifted us with many talents to use for His Kingdom, including, a Mental Health therapist, a Clinical specialist/pastor, a former supervisor/bus driver, a former Forensic scientist, a lab tech/future nursing student, a motivational speaker/actor/Kingdom rapper (Knowledge), and a fashion marketer/buyer/future esthetician. God uses our family, from all walks of life, as an example of the importance of love and unity, lacking in society today. Our mission is to bring the Kingdom of Heaven here on earth so that you can know that God does exist and truly cares about each and every one of you.
