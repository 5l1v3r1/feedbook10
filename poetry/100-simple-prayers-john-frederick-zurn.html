The book One Hundred Simple Prayers represents my beliefs and experiences that have evolved over many years. Addressed to God, these prayers are meant to be a source of inspiration and hope, for those of us who seek to live a spiritual life in a chaotic world. Each prayer has been written in simple, straight forward rhyme; but they are also subtle in meaning. They attempt to provide a fresh interpretation to ideas such as: despair, meditation, laughter and love.

Originally self-published, One Hundred Simple Prayers has sold over one hundred copies. It has been read by a wide variety of teenagers and adults, especially those seeking hope in times of doubt and loneliness. Two poems called "Children" and "Daily Prayer" have been published world-wide by the Salesian Missions.
