Its not to say, it could have been omitted but I know that God will not send me to damnation over a few curse words and if you have an opinion on it, I respect it and at the same time will happily tell you we all fall short. For no one sin is greater than another. Religion will never have me bound and I understand, love and appreciate the God I serve.
