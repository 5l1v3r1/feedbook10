Do you remember your high school sweetheart? What about your first breakup, and how you got over it? How rebellious you were as a teenager? How you finally knew you had true love? This collection of poetry will take you back to that time when you were first feeling love and how you evolved emotionally from the time you were a teenager to now. This book contains poetry written about everything from living to loving and heartache to moving on. And finally to finding the right one. So sit back, relax and allow these poems to take you back to those first true raw emotions uncorrupted by experience. Back to a time when your head was in the clouds and your heart was soaring. Enjoy your trip back to when you were young, in love and ready to take on the world.
