Poet, author of the love within my heart and the love of a hunt, Loynel Richards was born on this day September 13th, 1981. He grew up in Jamaica but migrated at a tender age of 21 to New York. He has one sister and two brothers. His mothers name is Clases Crandle and his fathers name is James Richards. He went to Browns Town Comprehensive High School. While attending Browns Town Comprehensive High School. He was prefect, student councilor, monitor, assistant monitor, form captain, assistant grand form captain, President of Geography club and house captain. What inspired him to write this book is because of the relationships he found himself in. He thought he found love. In the poems he feels hurt and abandoned and he still wants the person love but he is rejected and only gets more and more hurt. As he gets hurt and torn apart his world is broken and he seems to gravitate towards love. He wonders when will love comes his way, and this is a true story. Right now, it seems unending and he is searching for his soul mate.
