When Lifes Difficulties Obscure Your Path

New Poetry Book Offers Verses of Bliss and Healing

Nothing can minister to inner pain more than the right words that capture the hurting emotion and exhale if from your soul. Author BHL shares her own medicinal words in her latest book From God, Thru Me, To You, a collection of whole-hearted Bible referenced poetry that reaches deep down to the dark shadows of your life.

Like soothing salve to your soul, the poems of From God, Thru Me, To You penetrate the hardness of sorrow, loneliness, despair, and shame with the gentle harmony and words. When anxieties enslave you, turn to this unique collection of Spirituality inspired poems that offers encouragement, truth, honesty, and hope. Immerse your mind in this literary spa that holds a plethora of heart-warming, soul-reviving poems.

Author BHL knows how the condition and morale of the mind can determine the health of the whole body. In her own life, she has encountered a number of difficulties and was constantly harassed by gloomy thoughts. Fortunately she found the grace and love of God and has discovered that her life, with all its difficulties and joys, was Gods wonderful plan continually unfolding for her own good. In From God, Thru Me, To You, she now shares her personal compositions that speak of the heavenly comfort she found in troublesome times. Get a copy and be inspired today.
