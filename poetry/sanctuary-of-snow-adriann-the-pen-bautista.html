There is a certain peace found in the sound of silence that freshly fallen snow exudes a silence that wraps itself around you like a sanctuary.

Sanctuary of Snow is a collection of life poetry told vividly and boldly in Quadrants: Discovery, Love, Pain and finally Thoughts. Through the Pen you will experience first hand how she became aware of the need for a Sanctuary and then ultimately through beautifully hued free verse found herself despite the many challenges that life had presented.

S.O.S invites you to discover yourself in between the stanzas and beneath each layer of poetic sanctuary.
