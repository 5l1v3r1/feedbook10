This book is the story of how a man came to the United States, bringing with him his experience as a cook in the military. Not knowing what the future would hold, he soon met a young lady and took her as his wife. The two of them started down a road that would bring them great respect and love not only from their family but from each person who would come through the doors of their restaurant over the years. Although they are not with us today, Andy and Mary have left a special part of themselves, along with a legacy of dedication and hard work, showing hospitality for everyone and giving to the end.
