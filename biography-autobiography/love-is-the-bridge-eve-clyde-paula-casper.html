Paula and George Fraser both held good jobs at Paramount Pictures, and thanks to their show business exposure, a lot of connections were available. While working on Little Boy Lost, George ended up filming a scene outside an orphanage in Brussels, Belgium. That was the beginning of pursing their dream of getting a child.

The process with the governments was grueling. It was one disappointment after another—too many almosts. But nothing would deter Paula and George from getting the child they so desperately wanted. The first real glimmer of hope came in the mail, a picture of an almost eight-year-old girl. It was love at first sight.

Time seemed endless, the investigations never ending. How much longer before the little girl in the picture became theirs? No quitting, nothing short of death, would stop Paula and George from getting…me.

Love is the Bridge brings hope and ultimately proves that blood is not always thicker than water.
