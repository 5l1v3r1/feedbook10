Find your voice - and use it!

For many scrapbookers, simply recording names and dates is enough. For you, though, scrapbooking is so much more. It's the story - your story. But how do you really capture it on a page?

Your Words, Your Story takes the fear and confusion out of journaling by guiding you through the writing process with clear explanations and over 120 layouts featuring a variety of journaling styles.

Whatever your background and skill level, you can write meaningful journaling on a page. Your Words, Your Story helps you with:

  • Examples of different writing styles, from detailed narratives to witty observations to heartfelt letters and more.
  • "Problem Solved" features that provide a way around obstacles that keep you from journaling.
  • Inspiration for writing in creative styles like fairytale, science fiction and haiku.
  • Prompts and ideas to help you get your story down on a page.
Tell your own story as no one else can - and let Your Words, Your Story help you share it with the world.
