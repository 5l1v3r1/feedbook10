Furious with her husband, fifth grade teacher Laura Parker decides to go to Times Square by herself, in search of a single ticket to the Broadway play Hair.  Instead, an impetuous decision brings her to the hotel suite of Attorney Paul Bradley.  What she sees in the suite will lead to Bradley’s murder later that night.

When an innocent man is arrested for Bradley’s murder, Laura struggles with what to do.  Will she be able to live with herself if he goes to prison, knowing that she could have aided his defense or does she come forward and put her own life in danger?

The story takes place in the early 1970’s and brings to light a rash of untimely deaths that happened around the Watergate scandal.
