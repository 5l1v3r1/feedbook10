I like to write dirty stories. They are, for me, easy to write and fun too. I can see the couple trying to seduce each other and I can listen to their dialogue. And I can see both in bed and, on occasion, the stupidity and the fun of what they say and do. Writing these stories doesnt take much of my time; in a single day I can write two stories, revise and edit both stories before sending them to my typist. I wrote my autobiography in about four years, while the some thirty stories in this manuscript took no more than six weeks!
