In "Thicker Than Water," Tye and Wade are identical twin brothers; inseparable as boys, and now men, no one ever thought anything could come between them, but someone did. Dinah is the woman of every man's dreams. She's smart, sexy and down to earth. She's like one of the guys, so much so that next to his brother, Dinah is Wade's very best friend. After a night of alcohol and misinterpreted emotions, Wade and Dinah cross the friendship boundary line. Wade turns off his emotions when Tye’s strong attraction to Dinah connects the two. It’s a matter of time before Wade’s suppressed feelings get the best of him…and bring out the worst.
