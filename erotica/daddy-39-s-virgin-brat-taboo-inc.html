But when he sits me down and tells me I can confide in him, and that nothing we say or do will ever leave the room, well I guess the temptation was just too much for us. He raised an eyebrow and took in a deep breath. "Honestly? It feels great," he exhaled. "There's nothing quite like it." "Ohhhh, Daddy," I whined, kicking in to the floor with my heel. "Will it happen for me?" "Of course it will, sweetie. You just have to wait for the right person." "What if they never come along." He raised his hands and dropped them.
