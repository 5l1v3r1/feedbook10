Do you wish to be a happier and a more successful person? Not necessarily in the way that means headlines about you in the media, but, successful in your work and play, happier with the people around you.

You can learn to think more clearly, grew more industrious, get on better with other people, become generally a more complete, a more happy and successful man or woman.

Success comes if you keep using everything good you have in body, mind and spirit to achieve good results. Happiness comes as the result of continually striving to develop and use the qualities of your personality to this end.

There is only one person in the whole world who can do this for you - YOURSELF. Where do you begin? This series of 6 books sets out to show you what to do and how to go about doing it. By completing this study program you will realize your own strengths and weaknesses. Not only this, but, you will discover why you have such weaknesses and strengths and how you can develop them.

The first book in the series looks at you and where you are today. How you got to be the person you have become. How to overcome defects of character that you may possess. Discover your purpose in life.
