This will be hard and painful, tears will come to my eyes, emotions will be shattered and I will make you cry. But a duty I have toward my Maker and this is greater than I am, please forgive me darling but I have to find my rhyme. To rime and embrace greatness, to rime and reach the skies, to rime and find my Maker... to rime and make myself shine.
